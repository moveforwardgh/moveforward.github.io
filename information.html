About

Move Forward is an initiative that aims to contribute to the construction of a sustainable, equitable, global and scientific-technological future; bases that are necessary to solve the problems that we currently face as a civilization.

For this, here we try to develop theoretical elements that are necessary to address this new future, as well as to arrange, organize and deploy action around practical deployments necessary to go that way.

Thus, this portal and its complementary media will periodically present developments of different nature, which altogether will try to help shape a new future that overcomes the current stage of systemic crisis and eventual destruction.

For more information, comments, opinions and other related issues there is an open communication channel in social media.
